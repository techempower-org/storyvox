storyvox turns books, articles, and stories into beautiful audio — read out loud by your choice of on-device voices. No subscription. No account required.

Open the app and you've got an audiobook player ready to go. Tap to start. Skip ahead, jump back, fall asleep to a sleep timer, or play through Android Auto on the drive home.

═══════════

⊹ FREE BOOKS, GUIDES, AND THE WEB
Browse and listen to:
• Project Gutenberg classics (Pride and Prejudice, The Count of Monte Cristo, Frankenstein, thousands more)
• Standard Ebooks (Project Gutenberg reborn with proper typography)
• Wikipedia and Wikisource articles
• Royal Road web serials (sign in optional, for follows + reading lists)
• PLOS scientific journals (free open-access research)
• Hacker News stories
• arXiv research papers
• AO3 fanfiction (with your AO3 account)
• Any Notion workspace (paste your token, share a database, listen)
• Any RSS feed (your blogs, podcasts, anything)
• Any web article (paste a URL — magic-link reader extracts the text)
• Your own EPUB files (import from anywhere)

⊹ READ ALOUD BY ANY VOICE
Choose from dozens of free on-device voices:
• Crisp neural voices via Piper, Kokoro, and Kitten (run entirely on your phone — no internet needed once installed)
• Optional Microsoft Azure cloud voices if you want premium quality
• Per-book voice memory — assign different narrators to different stories
• Adjust speed, pitch, and pause-between-sentences live
• Pronunciation dictionary — teach the voice how to say specific names and words

⊹ BEAUTIFUL, ACCESSIBLE, AND CALM
Library Nocturne aesthetic — warm dark theme, brass accents, EB Garamond typography. Reduce motion, adjust font scale, and turn off animations from Settings → Accessibility. TalkBack-tested throughout.

⊹ PLAYS WHERE YOU PLAY
• Android Auto — listen on the drive home
• Lock-screen and notification controls
• Bluetooth, AirPods, car stereo — all the audio routes
• Sleep timer with shake-to-extend
• Home-screen widget — see what's playing without unlocking
• Auto-advance through chapters, with smart prerendering so the next chapter is ready before you need it

⊹ CLOUD SYNC (OPTIONAL)
Sign in to sync your library, follows, reading positions, bookmarks, and voice settings across all your devices. Powered by InstantDB — end-to-end secure, no ads, your data is yours. The app works fully offline if you'd rather not.

⊹ FOR PEOPLE WHO LIKE TO LISTEN
storyvox is built for audiobook readers, drivers, walkers, accessibility users, busy parents, students, and anyone who wants to hear instead of read. Open-source, no ads, no tracking, no upsells.
